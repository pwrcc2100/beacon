### 4. How Beacon Works
1. **Visibility of Risk Indicators** – Early identification of psychosocial risk indicators to enable faster response.
2. **Simple Feedback Channel** – Weekly SMS/web pulse with an easy interface for flagging issues.
3. **Beyond Traditional Metrics** – Goes beyond LTIFR; captures emotional sentiment and psychological safety.
4. **Trusted Communication** – Regular, trusted channel for employee feedback.

### 5. Pulse Survey Overview
- **Format:** Fast weekly pulse with 3 response options per question delivered via SMS or link.
- **Purpose:** Detect early stages of psychosocial pressure and workplace conditions.

### 6. Five Core Questions (Why They Matter)
1. **Workload & Pressure** – Detects job demand spikes.
2. **Support & Communication** – Shows whether teams feel backed by leaders.
3. **Clarity & Planning** – Identifies confusion, rework, coordination issues.
4. **Team Climate & Respect** – Captures psychological safety signals.
5. **General Wellbeing / Sentiment** – Rapid view of how people feel about work this week.

> Note: Each question maps to ISO 45003 risk factors and evidence-based psychosocial drivers.

### 7. Index Creation Process
1. Raw responses
2. Cleaned & de-identified
3. Weighted scoring model applied
4. Dashboard generated with trend analysis
5. Beacon Index (0–100%) for teams and overall organisation

### 8. Leadership Feedback Loop
- **Insight:** Leaders see hotspots, comments, trends, weekly movement.
- **Outcome:** Early detection allows targeted support; focus on conditions rather than individuals.

### 9. Leadership Outcomes
- Identify teams under pressure
- Monitor communication/support quality
- See planning and clarity breakdowns
- Track recovery/decline week-to-week
- Measure initiative impact
- Understand psychosocial trends across sites

### 10. Business Impact
- Lower psychosocial risk and ISO 45003 alignment
