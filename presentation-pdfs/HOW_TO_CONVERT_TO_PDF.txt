METHOD 2: Use Online Converter
====================================================================

Fast and free online tools:

1. **Sejda** (Recommended)
   https://www.sejda.com/html-to-pdf
   - Upload HTML files
   - Download PDFs

2. **CloudConvert**
   https://cloudconvert.com/html-to-pdf
   - Batch convert multiple files
   - High quality output

3. **PDFCrowd**
   https://pdfcrowd.com/html-to-pdf/
   - Professional formatting
   - Free for basic use

====================================================================
METHOD 3: Install LaTeX for Direct PDF Conversion
====================================================================

If you want to convert directly from Markdown to PDF:

1. Install BasicTeX:
   - Download from: https://www.tug.org/mactex/morepackages.html
   - Or use Homebrew: brew install --cask basictex

2. After installation, restart your terminal and run:
   cd /Users/peta/Documents/Beacon/webapp/beacon
   ./scripts/convert-to-pdf.sh
