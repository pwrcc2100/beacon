## Rationale

The new question is:
- More specific: "this week" focuses on recent timeframe
- Shorter and clearer
- Aligns with weekly pulse survey frequency
- Less formal/corporate language

## Response Options (Unchanged)
- ✅ Good – I'm doing well
- 😐 Okay – Getting by
- ❌ Not great – I'm struggling
